2. Narrative & Lore

Backstory

Yoi, a teenage girl, has fallen into a deep coma following a suicide attempt. Unknown to her, her subconscious has created a surreal dream world shaped by her emotions. Ren, her deceased father, is mysteriously summoned to this world and must navigate its dreamlike environments to find scattered Tears—emotional shards of Yoi's life and trauma.

Progression

Each Tear unlocks a part of Yoi's memory, gradually revealing the circumstances that led to her condition. Collecting all Tears allows Ren to confront the final emotional trial, resulting in Yoi waking up somehow aware that her father saved her from the depths of her own mind.

3. Gameplay Mechanics

Abilities Unlocked by Tears

Joy
Dash

A quick burst of movement to evade attacks or traverse gaps in the dreamscape.

Anger
Smash

A heavy AOE attack that can break obstacles. Costs no mana, fueled by pure rage.

Fear
Shadow Ball

A ranged projectile of dark energy that consumes a small amount of mana.

Grief
Frenzy

Sacrifice health for a temporary, high-damage state. A risky, powerful move.

Love
Passive

Unlocked at the end. A symbolic ability that completes the narrative arc.

4. World Design

Symbolic Environments

The dream world is built from Yoi's subconscious. Each area is a manifestation of her emotional state.

  • Forests: Representing confusion and moments of peace.
  • Caves: Dark internal struggles and hidden fears.
  • Ruins: Forgotten memories and the decay of happiness.
  • Dungeons: Emotional prisons where trauma is locked away.
